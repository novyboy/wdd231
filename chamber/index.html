Current Events

Honolulu Small Business Growth Summit Opens at Ala Moana Hotel

Date: July 12, 2026

The Chamber launched its annual Small Business Growth Summit, bringing together more than 300 local entrepreneurs for workshops on digital marketing, tourism trends, and AI‑powered business tools.

Key highlight: A panel on “Sustainable Tourism for 2027” featuring local restaurateurs and eco‑tour operators.

Learn more: Small Business Summit

Waikiki Retail Revitalization Program Announces 22 New Storefront Grants

Date: July 8, 2026

The Chamber partnered with the City & County of Honolulu to award grants to small retailers opening or renovating storefronts in Waikiki.

The program focuses on locally owned shops, cultural artisans, and food vendors.

Learn more: Retail Revitalization Program

Chamber Launches “Hire Hawaiʻi” Workforce Initiative

Date: July 3, 2026

A new workforce development program connects local employers with job seekers through training, resume workshops, and monthly hiring fairs.

The initiative emphasizes hospitality, healthcare, and tech roles.

Learn more: Hire Hawaiʻi Initiative
